Burrito

Description

A very simple but tasty burrito that will blow your tastebuds away!

    Ingredients:
  • Tortillas
  • Mexican Rice
  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Guacamole
    Steps to make:
  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Toast the tortillas
  4. Put the burrito together
  5. Eat to yoru hearts content!
