Nachos

Description

Cheesy, meaty nachos that will put you on heart attack alert.

    Ingredients:
  • Tortilla chips
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Tomatos
  • Chicken
  • Steak
  • Peppers
    Steps:
  1. Cook meat together
  2. Dice tomatos
  3. Cut up peppers
  4. Heat up chips
  5. Put everything together
  6. Mix and put in over
  7. Eat your heart out!
