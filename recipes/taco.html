Taco

Description

Simple juicy beef tacos that will make the whole family happy. With shredded cheese and diced tomatos. Make it even better with your own ingredients.

    Ingredients:
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatos
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Taco Shells
  • Taco seasoning
    Steps:
  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Dice the Tomatos
  3. Toast the Taco Shells
  4. Season the cooked meat
  5. Assemble the Tacos
  6. Eat your heart out!
